Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is EduKar really free?

Yes! EduKar is 100% free for all students. There are no hidden charges for browsing universities, applying for scholarships, or tracking your applications.

How do I apply to a university?

Simply create an account, upload your educational documents (matric, inter, etc.), browse universities, select a program, and click "Apply Now". It's that simple!

Are my documents safe?

Absolutely. We use secure encrypted storage for all your documents. Your personal data and academic records are fully protected and never shared without your permission.

Can I track my application status?

Yes! You get real-time notifications and status updates for every application — from Applied → Under Review → Test → Interview → Selected/Rejected.

Which universities are available?

We list top universities across Sindh and Pakistan including University of Sindh, MUET, NED, SZABIST, and many more. New universities are added regularly.
